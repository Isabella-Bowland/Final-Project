How much do you know about immigration?

Immigration is a hot topic in today's political climate with a lot of misinformation permeating throughout the country. Whether you live up in Maine or in the borderlands of the southwest, understanding our country's past, present, and future with regards to immigration is the first step towards becoming a more informed voter!

MYTH OR FACT

Residing in the United States without documentation is a crime.

Immigrants drive down the wages of American workers.

When immigrants without documentation are caught, most are immediatley and quickly deported.

Despite what our current president says, there is already a wall on the US-Mexico border.
